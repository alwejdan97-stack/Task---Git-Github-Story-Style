In the quiet village of Elderglen, where mist curled lazily around ancient oaks, a young villager named Liora stumbled upon something that would unravel the world she thought she knew.
Hidden beneath a loose floorboard in her late grandfather’s cottage lay a weathered map, its edges brittle and ink shimmering faintly as if alive.
Strange symbols marked paths no one had ever spoken of, leading deep into the forbidden Whispering Woods. 
As Liora traced the lines with trembling fingers, a soft murmur seemed to rise from the parchment itself—calling her toward a destiny far greater, and far more dangerous, than she could imagine.
-------------------------------------------------
Elara spread the fragile map across her small wooden table, weighing its curling corners down with smooth river stones. The ink shimmered faintly in the candlelight, forming symbols she did not recognize, yet somehow felt she understood. 
A narrow path twisted through a dense cluster of trees drawn at the center—surely the Whispering Woods. As her fingers traced the route, the markings seemed to shift ever so slightly, as if guiding her touch. A faint whisper brushed her ears, too soft to make out, yet impossible to ignore.

She pulled her cloak tighter around her shoulders, her mind racing between caution and curiosity. No one in the village dared enter those woods, yet the map felt like it had chosen her.
With a steady breath, Elara folded the parchment carefully and tucked it close to her heart. Before doubt could take hold, she stepped beyond the safety of her home and toward the shadowed tree line, determined to uncover the secrets waiting within.
------------------------------------------------
Elara hesitated at the edge of the Whispering Woods, where sunlight thinned into pale strands and shadows stretched like watchful figures. The air grew cooler as she stepped forward, carrying a faint murmur that seemed to drift between the trees.
Her heart pounded with a mix of fear and wonder, each step deeper making the village behind her feel impossibly distant. Though unease prickled at her skin, a stronger pull urged her onward, as if the forest itself had been waiting for her arrival.
------------------------------------------------
Just as the whisper faded, Elara blinked and shook her head, wondering why, of all things, she had suddenly started craving something as strange and distant as pizza.
