In the quiet village of Elderglen, where mist curled lazily around ancient oaks, a young villager named Liora stumbled upon something that would unravel the world she thought she knew.
Hidden beneath a loose floorboard in her late grandfather’s cottage lay a weathered map, its edges brittle and ink shimmering faintly as if alive.
Strange symbols marked paths no one had ever spoken of, leading deep into the forbidden Whispering Woods. 
As Liora traced the lines with trembling fingers, a soft murmur seemed to rise from the parchment itself—calling her toward a destiny far greater, and far more dangerous, than she could imagine.
