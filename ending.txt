At the heart of the Whispering Woods, Elara finally unraveled the secrets of the ancient map, revealing a hidden spring whose waters shimmered with quiet magic. When she returned to her village, the once-fading lands began to flourish again—crops grew tall, rivers ran clear, and hope filled every home.
With Shadow by her side, Elara was no longer just a curious villager, but a guardian of newfound prosperity, her courage forever woven into the story of the woods.
