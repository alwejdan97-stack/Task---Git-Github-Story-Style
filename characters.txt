Elara, a spirited young villager with bright, searching eyes, is known for her quiet courage and endless curiosity.
When she discovers a mysterious, timeworn map hidden away, her heart races with excitement. Though uncertain of its dangers, she feels irresistibly drawn to the adventure it promises.
---------------------------------------------------------------
Shadow is a sleek, silver-eyed wolf with fur as dark as midnight, moving silently through the forest beside Elara. Fiercely loyal and deeply intuitive, he senses danger before it comes. 
Though wild by nature, his calm presence and unwavering devotion make him her most trusted companion.
