At the heart of the forest, Elara finally reached the hidden sanctum marked on the map. A circular chamber opened before her, illuminated by a soft, golden light that seemed to come from nowhere and everywhere at once.

In the center stood a stone pedestal, and upon it rested a small, ancient object—the source of the map’s power.

As Elara approached, the whispers grew silent.

The moment she touched the object, a flood of visions filled her mind—memories not her own, fragments of the past, and a warning:

Some knowledge was never meant to be found.
