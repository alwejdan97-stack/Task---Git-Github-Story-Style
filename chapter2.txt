The deeper Elara walked into the Whispering Woods, the louder the whispers became. They no longer sounded like wind but like distant voices calling her name. She turned sharply at every sound, her heart racing, unsure if she was being guided or watched.

A sudden rustle in the bushes made her freeze. From the shadows emerged a wolf—silent, steady, and unafraid. Instead of attacking, it simply looked at her. Elara felt an unusual calm wash over her. Somehow, she knew this creature meant no harm.

“Shadow,” she whispered without knowing why.

The wolf stepped forward, as if accepting the name, and began walking ahead. After a brief hesitation, Elara followed. For the first time since entering the woods, she didn’t feel alone.
